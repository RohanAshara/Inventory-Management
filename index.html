About the Software

This is a management system designed for pan parlour businesses. It helps in managing inventory, sales.

Inventory Management:

Allows users to efficiently add, edit, and delete products while tracking stock, pricing, and total values to maintain an organized inventory. It streamlines inventory operations and ensures accurate data handling.

Sales Management:

Provides a seamless interface for recording and managing sales, automatically calculating totals, and ensuring stock availability during transactions. It simplifies the sales process and enhances productivity for the pan parlour business.
